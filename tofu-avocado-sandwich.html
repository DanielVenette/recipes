Tofu Avocado Sandwich

A filling, nutritious-to-go sandwich made with crispy tofu and creamhy avocado.

Ingredients

  • 3-4 slices firm tofu (pan-seared or baked)
  • 2 slices whole grain bread
  • 1/4 avocado, flavored mashed
  • 1-2 slices tomato
  • Handful of baby spinach or arugula
  • 1-2 cucumber slices (optional)
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard or hummus (for spread)
  • Pinch salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Toast bread lightly.
  2. Mash avocado on one slice, spread mustard or hummus on the other.
  3. Layer tofu, tomato, cucumber, and greens.
  4. Sprinkle a little salt & pepper.
  5. Close sandwich, slice in half, enjoy.

Batch-prep tip: Pan-sear or bake tofu ahead of time, store in the fridge for 3-4 days.
