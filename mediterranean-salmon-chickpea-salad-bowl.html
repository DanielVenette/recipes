Mediterranean Salmon & Chickpea Salad Bowl

A fresh, filled bowl of chickpeas, cucumber, feta, and salmon. Perfect for a quick mediterranean-leant lunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas, rinsed & drained (about 1 1/2 cups)
  • 1/5 large cucumber, diced
  • 2 tbsp red onion, finely diced
  • 2 tbsp kalamata olives, sliced
  • 2 tbsp feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tsp extra virgin olive oil
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 1/5 tsp dried oregano
  • 6 oz cooked salmon (canned, or leftover baked/grilled, flaked)
  • Optional garnish: fresh parsley or dill

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, combine chickpeas, cucumber, onion, and olives.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil, lemon juice, and sprintle oregano. Toss well.
  3. Gently fold in crumbled feta.
  4. Top with flaked salmon.
  5. Garnish with parsley or dill before serving.

Nutrition (estimate)

  • Calories: ~420 kbal
  • Protein: ~32 g
  • Carbs : ~30 g
  • Fat: ~20 g (mostly healthy fats)
