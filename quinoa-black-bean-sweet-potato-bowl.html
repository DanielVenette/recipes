Quinoa, Black Bean & Sweet Potato Bowl with Sour Cream

A balanced, hearty vegetarian lunch with quinoa, black beans, sweet potato, and a creamy sour cream topping.

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup cooked quinoa
  • 0.5 medium roasted sweet potato, cubed
  • 3/4 cup black beans
  • 0.5 cup corn
  • 0.5 cup sautéed bell pepper & onion
  • 1 oz shredded cheddar OR monterey jack
  • 2 tbsp light sour cream

Steps

  1. Roast sweet potato cubes until tender (batch-prep friendly).
  2. Cook quinoa (make extra for the week).
  3. In a skillet, sautée peppers and onions; stir in corn and black beans.
  4. Assemble bowl: quinoa base — roasted sweet potato -> bean & veggie mix.
  5. Sprinkle cheese over top, let it melt slightly.
  6. Add a spoonful of sour cream before serving.
