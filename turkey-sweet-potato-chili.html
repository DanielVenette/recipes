Turkey & Sweet Potato Chili

Yield: 6 servings · Total: ~40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs ground turkey
  • 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and diced small
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, rinsed and drained
  • 2 cans (14.5 oz each) diced tomatoes
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1–2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot; sauté onion and bell pepper 3–4 minutes.
  2. Add ground turkey; cook until browned and cooked through.
  3. Stir in sweet potatoes, black beans, kidney beans, diced tomatoes, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Bring to a simmer and cook 20–25 minutes, stirring occasionally, until sweet potatoes are tender.
  5. Taste and adjust seasoning. Portion into 6 containers for make-ahead dinners.
