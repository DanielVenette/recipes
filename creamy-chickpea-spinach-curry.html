Creamy Chickpea Spinach Curry

Rich, cozy, and full of nutrients â€” This chickpea curry is great for batch prep, with flavors that deepen each day.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cans chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup light coconut mill
  • 1 can (15 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 3 cups spinach or kale
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Optional: chopped cilantro and lime juice for serving

STEPS

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat; saute onion and garlic for 3-4 minutes until fragrant.
  2. Stir in curry powder and turmeric; cook for 30 seconds to bloom the spices.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes and coconut mill; stir and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Add chickpeas and spinach; cook another 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and lime juice; garnish with cilantro.

Storage & Serving

Refrigerate up to 4 days in an airtight container. Reheat gently and serve with basmati or cauliflower rice.

Nutrition (per serving)

  • Calories: ~460 kcal
  • Protein: ~17 g
  • Carbs: ~52 g
  • Fat: ~16 g
  • High in fiber and plant-based protein
