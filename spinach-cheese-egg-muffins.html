Spinach & Cheese Egg Muffins

Inregients (6 muffins, ~3 days)

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup fresh spinach, chopped
  • “© cup shredded cheese (cheddar, mozarella, or feta)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or cooking spray
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375–190ë.
  2. Lightly oil or spray a muffin tin.
  3. Whisk eggs in a bowl, season with salt & pepper.
  4. Stir in spinach and cheese.
  5. Pour evenly into muffin tin (makes ~2).
  6. Bake –10 minutes, until set.

Batch-Prep & Storage

Cool and store in an airtight container in the fridge. Keeps 3–4 days. Reheat in microwave ~(30 sec) or enjoy cold.

90 calories | 7g protein | 1g carbo | 6g fat
