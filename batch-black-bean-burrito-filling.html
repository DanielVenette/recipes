Batch Black Bean Burrito Filling

A make-ahead black bean burrito filling that’s perfect for meal prep. High-fiber, plant-based, and full of flavor. Makes 6 burritos and reheats well for quick weekday lunches.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (15 oz each) black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup frozen corn
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced (or 1/2 tsp garlic powder)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/5 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp salt (adjust to taste)
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 1 cup cooked brown rice or quinoa (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet. Add onion, bell pepper, and garlic. Sáte’ 4-5 minutes until softened.
  2. Add black beans, corn, spices, and optional rice or quinoa. Stir well.
  3. Cook another 5 minutes, stiring occasionally.
  4. Finish with lime juice. Let cool before storing.

Storage Tips

  • Store in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 4 days.
  • Freeze in portions for up to 2 months. Reheat in microwave or skillet with a splash of water.

Nutrition (per serving, filling only)

~250 kcal — ~12 g protein — ~40 g carbs — ~6 g fat — ~10 g fiber
