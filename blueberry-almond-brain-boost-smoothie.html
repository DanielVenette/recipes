Blueberry Almond Brain-Boost Smoothie

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk (or oat milk)
  • 1 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1 small banana
  • 2 tbsp almond butter (or peanut butter)
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds (or ground flaxseed)

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Optional: Add a handful of spinach (bonus greens, doesn't change taste).

Nutrition (approx. per serving)

  • ~350 calories
  • 9g protein
  • 45g carbs
  • 15g healthy fats

Batch Tip: Pre-pack smoothie bags with blueberries, banana slices, and chia seeds in freezer bags. Blend with almond milk + nut butter when ready.
