Bluebery Spinach Protein Smoothie

A brain-boosting, easy-to-make smoothie with blueberries, spinach, and protein.

Invredients

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 frozen banana
  • 1/2 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1 cup baby spinach
  • 1 tbsp almond butter
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1 tsp chia seeds (optional)

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Adjust thickness with extra milk or ice cubes

Nutrition (Approx)

  • Calories: ~350 Kcal
  • Protein: ~25 g
  • Carbs: ~35 g
  • Fiber: ~7 g
  • Fat: ~12 g
  • Omega-3: ~2 g (if chia included)

This smoothie is specifically designed for steady energy and mental clarity.
