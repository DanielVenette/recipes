Turkey & Quinoa Veggie Bake

Yield: 4 servings · Total: ~30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs ground turkey
  • 1 cup dry quinoa (yields ~3 cups cooked)
  • 2 cups spinach (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 cup mushrooms, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes, no salt added
  • 1–2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2–3 cloves garlic (or 1 tsp minced)
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Optional: 1/2 cup feta or shredded mozzarella

Instructions

  1. Cook quinoa according to package (or use pre-cooked).
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet; sauté onion and garlic 2–3 minutes.
  3. Add ground turkey; cook until browned and cooked through.
  4. Stir in mushrooms, spinach, diced tomatoes, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper; cook 3–5 minutes until vegetables soften.
  5. Combine turkey mixture with cooked quinoa. Taste and adjust seasoning.
  6. Optional: top with feta or mozzarella before serving or packing.
  7. Portion into 4 containers for make-ahead dinners.
