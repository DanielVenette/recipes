Mediterranean Chickpea Wrap

Q a healthy, vegetarian-protein wrap you can assemble fresh in minutes.

Ingredients (per wrap):

  • 1 whole grain tortilla
  • &fraction; cup smashed chickpeas (or roasted chickpeas)
  • 2 Tbsp hummus
  • Baby spinach or arugula
  • Cucumber slices
  • Tomato slices or cherry halves
  • Crumbled feta (optional)

Steps:

  1. Spread hummus across the tortilla.
  2. Add smashed chickpeas and veggies.
  3. Roll tightly and enjoy!

Batch-Prep Tip

Roast a high quantity of chickpeas in advance with olive oil, cumin, and paprika; store in the fridge for quick assembly.
