Spinach & Berry Smoothie

A quick and energizing smoothie you can whip up in 5 minutes.

Ingredients (1 serving):

  • 1 cup fresh spinach (packed)
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk (or other milk of choice)
  • ‘.5 cup frozen mixed berries (blueberries, raspberries, blackberries)
  • ‘.5 frozen banana (for creaminess & sweetness)
  • 1 Tbsp almond butter (or peanut butter)
  • 1 tsp chia seeds (optional boost for fiber & omega-3s)

Steps:

  • Add all ingredients to a blender.
  • Blend until smooth and creamy.
  • Adjust thickness with extra milk or ice.

Optional batch tip:

Pre-portion spinach + fruit into freezer bags for quick grab-and-blend smoothies all week.

Nutrition (approx):

280 Kcal, 8g Protein, 38g Carbs, 11g Fat, 8g Fiber
