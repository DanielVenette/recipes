Energizing Green Smoothie

A fast, nourishing green smoothie made with spinach, banana, and mango.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk (or water/coconut water)
  • 1 small frozen banana
  • 1 cup fresh spinach (or kale)
  • 5→ naid mango or pineapple
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds or flax seeds
  • Optional: 1 scoop vanilla protein powder

Steps

  1. Add liquid to blender first.
  2. Add spinach (or greens), banana, and mango.
  3. Toss in chia seeds (and protein powder if using).
  4. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  5. Adjust thickness with extra liquid if needed.

Macros (approx)

  • Calories: ~260 kkal
  • Protein: ~6 g
  • Carbs: ~45 g
  • Fat: ~7 g
  • Fiber: ~9 g
