Mediterranean Chickpea Wraps

Quick, plant-based wraps packed with chickpeas, veggies, and Mediterranean flavors. Perfect for meal-prep: 5 minutes to assemble, stores well for 3 days. ~500 calories each.

Ingredients (3 wraps)

  • 3 whole wheat tortillas (large, #200 cal each)
  • 2 cans chickpeas, drained & rinsed (~3 cups cooked)
  • 3 tbsp hummus ~1 tbsp per wrap
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • – · red onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cups baby spinach (1 cup per wrap)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil *1 dbsp per wrap)
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Steps (5 minutes)

  1. In a bowl, mash chickpeas roughly with a fork (leave some whole for texture).
  2. Mix in olive oil, lemon juice, oregano, salt & pepper.
  3. Lay out tortillas and spread 1 tbsp hummus on each.
  4. Add spinach, cucumber, bell pepper, tomato, onion, then top with chickpea misture.
  5. Wrap tightly. Store in the fridge up to 3 days.

Nutrition (per wrap, approx)

  • Calories: ~500
  • Protein: 16g
  • Carbs: 64g
  • Fat: 18g
  • Fiber: 14g
