Kiwi Ginger Refresher

A refreshing and zingy green smoothie to brighten your day

Ingredients

  • 1 kiwi (peeled)
  • 1/2 green apple
  • 1/2 cucumber
  • 1 tsp fresh ginger
  • 1 handful spinach
  • 1 cup water

Steps

  1. Blend all ingredients until smooth.
  2. Optional: strain if you want it more juice-like.
