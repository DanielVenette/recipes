Green Power Breakfast Smoothie

A 740-calorie breakfast smoothie with fozen spinach, penut butter, oats, protein powder, and chia & flax seeds.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 medium banana
  • 1/2 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1/2 cup frozen spinach (about 2 oz / 60 g)
  • 2 tbsp natural peanut butter
  • 0.5/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1 tbsp ground flaxseed
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds

Steps

  1. Add almond milk, banana, blueberries, and frozen spinach to blender. Blend until smooth.
  2. Add peanut butter, oats, protein powder, flaxseed, and chia seeds. Blend again until creamy.
  3. Adjust thickness with a splash of almond milk or water if needed.

Nutrition

Approx: 740 kal, protein: 42 g, carbs: 78 g, fat: 26 g
