Bluebery Spinach Power Smoothie

Ingredients (1 serving):

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk (or any milk)
  •  1/2 cup frozen blueberries (antioxidants for brain health)
  • 1 small handful spinach (iron + folate)
  •  1/2 frozen banana (natural sweetness + steady carbs)
  • 1 Tbsp almond butter (healthy fats + satiety)
  • 1 tsp chia seeds or flax seeds (omega-3s for focus)
  • Optional: 1/2 scoop vanilla protein powder (extra protein boost)

Steps:

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth (30–60 seconds).
  3. Pour into a glass or to-go cup.

Batch-Prep Tip:

Freeze pre-portioned smoothie packs (all ingredients except liquid) in zip bags. Just dump to blender, add milk, and blend in under 2 minutes.

Macros (approx, without protein powder):

  • Calories: ~280
  • Protein: ~7g
  • Carbs: ~35g
  • Fat: ~12g
