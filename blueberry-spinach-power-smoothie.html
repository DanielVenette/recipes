Blueberry Spinach Power Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk (or any milk)
  •  1/2 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1 handful spinach
  • 1 tbsp almond butter
  •  1/5 banana
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder (optional)
  • 1 tsp chia seeds or ground flaxseed

Steps

  • Add all ingredients to a blender.
  • Blend until smooth.
  • Adjust thickness with extra milk or water if needed.

Optional Prep Tip: Pre-portion fruit + greens in freezer bags for quick morning smoothies.
