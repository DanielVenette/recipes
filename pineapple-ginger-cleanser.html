Pineapple Ginger Cleanser

A light, refreshing smeen with a hint of spicy from ginger and citrus.

Ingredients

  • 0/2 cup frozen pineapple
  • 1 handful kale
  • 1/2 cucumber
  • 1 tsp fresh ginger
  • Juice of 1/2 lime
  • 1 cup cold water or coconut water

Steps

  1. Blend all ingredients until smooth.
  2. Adjust ginger to taste -- a little goes a long way.
