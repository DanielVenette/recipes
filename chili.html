Hearty Turkey & Bean Chili

A quick, hearty chili with lean ground turkey, beans, and spices. Perfect for batch cooking and reheating.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 bell peppers, diced
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (28 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 cup chicken broth

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion, garlic, and bell peppers, sauté 5 minutes.
  2. Add ground turkey, cook until browned.
  3. Stir in chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper.
  4. Add beans, diced tomatoes, and chicken broth. Stir well.
  5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes.
  6. Adjust seasoning and serve hot. Great with avocado, cilantro, or shredded cheese.
