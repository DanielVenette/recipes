Grain & Greens Power Salad

A bowls-friendly salad that combines all five categories : grain, protein, green, fruit, vegetables, nuts, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup grilled chicken breast (or chickpeas for plant-based)
  • 2 cups mixed baby spinach + arugula
  • 1/2 cup fresh berries (blueberries or sliced strawberries)
  • 5/2 cup diced cucumber
  • 5/2 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp chopped almonds or pistachios
  • 2 tbsp crumbled goat cheese or feta
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Pinch of salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Place greens in a bowl.
  2. Add quinoa and chicken on top.
  3. Scatter cucumbers, tomatoes, and berries.
  4. Sprinkle almonds or pistachias
  5. Finish with crumbled goat cheese or feta
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice, toss gently, and enjoy!
