Chickpea & Veggie Power Bake

Healthy batch-cook meal perfect for 3 days of high-protein, plant-based leftovers. Quick to prep, full of color, and easy to reheat.

Ingredients

  • 3 cans (15 oz each) chickpeas, rinsed & drained
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 zoocchini, chopped
  • 1 small red onion, diced
  • 2 cups baby spinach or kale, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta or nutritional yeast (optional)
  • Optional: squeeze of lemon or drizzle of tahini before serving

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400F (200CC).
  2. Toss chickpeas and veggies with olive oil and spices.
  3. Spread evenly on a lined baking sheet or casserole dish.
  4. Roast for 25 minutes, stirring halfway, until golden and tender.
  5. Top with feta or drizzle of tahini and lemon before serving.

Storage & Serving

Store in an airtight container up to 4 days. Reheat with a spllash of water or enjoy cold over greens or grains. Excellent for lunches or quick dinners.

Nutrition (per serving)

  • Calories: ~420 kcal
  • Protein: ~18 g
  • Carbs: ~48 g
  • Fat: ~14 g
  • High in fiber & plant-based protein
