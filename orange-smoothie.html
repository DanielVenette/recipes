Orange Smoothie

A creamy, fresh healthy smoothie made with orange, banana, yogurt, and milk in under 5 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 large orange (peeled, seeded)
  • ‖ banana (frozen if possible, for creaminess)
  • µ cup Greek yogurt (or dairy-free alternative)
  • µ cup unsweetened almond milk (or other milk)
  • 1 tsp honey or maple syrup (optional, sweetness)
  • A few ice cubes

Steps

  • Add orange, banana, yogurt, milk, and optional sweetener to a blender.
  • Blend until smooth.
  • Add ice cubes and blend again for a chilled smoothie.
