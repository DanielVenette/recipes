Mediterranean Hummus Wrap

Ingredients (1 serving):

  • 1 whole grain tortilla or wrap
  • 3 tbsp hummus
  •  cup baby spinach
  •  cup cucumber, sliced
  •   cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 tbsp feta cheese (optional)
  • 1 tbsp olives, sliced
  • Drizzle of olive oil + squeeze of lemon

Steps:

  1. Spread hummus on tortilla.
  2. Layer spinach, cucumber, tomatoes, feta, and olives.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil + lemon
  4. Roll up and slice in half.
